VIBE MODE - PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Vibe Mode is a productivity Chrome extension that helps you focus by blocking distracting websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Vibe Mode operates entirely locally on your device.

Data Storage

Vibe Mode stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Settings: Your timer duration preferences, theme selection, and notification preferences
  • Blocked Sites: Your list of websites to block during focus sessions
  • Session Statistics: Count of completed sessions, streak data, and session timestamps
  • Active Session State: Current timer status and end time

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Permissions

Vibe Mode requires the following permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your settings and session statistics locally
  • tabs: To detect and block access to distracting websites
  • declarativeNetRequest: To block website requests during focus sessions
  • notifications: To notify you when focus sessions complete (if enabled)
  • alarms: To manage timer functionality and session tracking
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>): To block any website you add to your block list

Third-Party Services

Vibe Mode does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The extension operates completely offline once installed.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties because we don't collect any user information.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage APIs. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

Vibe Mode is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Vibe Mode, please create an issue on our GitHub repository or contact The Vibe Coder.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can view all stored data through Chrome's developer tools
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the extension
  • You can export/import your settings through the extension's settings page
  • You have complete control over your data at all times
